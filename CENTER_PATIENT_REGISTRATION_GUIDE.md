# Center Patient Registration Guide

## Overview
Centers can now register walk-in patients directly from their dashboard. This feature allows screening centers to quickly onboard patients who visit in person.

## How to Access
1. Log in to your center account
2. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Register Patient** (Quick Action)
3. Or use the sidebar: **Register Patient**

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Fill Patient Information
Complete the registration form with the following details:

**Personal Information:**
- Full Name *
- Email Address * (patient will use this to log in)
- Phone Number *
- Date of Birth *
- Gender * (Male/Female)

**Location:**
- State *
- Local Government Area *

*All fields marked with * are required*

### Step 2: Submit Registration
- Click **"Register Patient"** button
- System automatically generates a secure temporary password
- Format: `ZC` + 8 random characters (e.g., `ZCAB12CD34`)

### Step 3: Share Credentials
After successful registration, you'll see a success screen with:

**Patient Credentials:**
- Patient Name
- Email Address
- Temporary Password

**Actions Available:**
- **Copy Credentials** - Copies email and password to clipboard
- **Print** - Opens print dialog to print credentials
- **Register Another Patient** - Returns to registration form

## Important Notes

### For Center Staff:
1. ✅ Write down or print credentials immediately
2. ✅ Hand credentials to the patient securely
3. ✅ Inform patient to change password after first login
4. ✅ Verify email address is correct before submitting

### For Patients:
1. Patient can log in at the website using provided credentials
2. Patient should change password immediately after first login
3. Patient can now book appointments online
4. Patient receives email verification link

## Security Features
- Temporary passwords are randomly generated
- Passwords are hashed before storage
- Email verification required for full account access
- Patients prompted to change password on first login

## Common Use Cases

### Walk-in Patient
1. Patient visits center without prior appointment
2. Center staff registers patient on the spot
3. Patient receives credentials
4. Patient can book future appointments online

### Phone Registration
1. Patient calls center to inquire about services
2. Center staff can register patient over the phone
3. Credentials sent via SMS or email
4. Patient can complete booking online

### Community Outreach
1. Center conducts health screening event
2. Staff registers multiple patients on-site
3. Each patient receives their credentials
4. Follow-up appointments can be booked online

## Troubleshooting

### Email Already Registered
- Error: "Email already registered"
- Solution: Patient may already have an account. Ask them to log in or use password reset.

### Invalid Phone Number
- Error: "Please enter a valid phone number"
- Solution: Ensure phone number has at least 7 digits. Include country code if international.

### State/LGA Not Available
- Solution: Select state first, then LGA dropdown will populate with available options.
