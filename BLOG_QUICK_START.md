## 🎯 What You Can Do

### View the Blog
1. Open browser to `http://localhost:3002/blog`
2. See featured article in hero section
3. Browse other articles in grid below
4. Click "Read" to view full articles

### Manage Content (Admin)
1. Login to admin panel
2. Go to `/admin/blog`
3. View all posts (published and drafts)
4. Edit, delete, or publish/unpublish posts
5. Create new posts with the "New Post" button

### Add New Articles
1. Click "New Post" in admin panel
2. Fill in:
   - Title
   - Excerpt
   - Content (supports Markdown)
   - Cover Image URL
   - Select categories
   - Check "Publish immediately" or save as draft
3. Click "Create Post"
